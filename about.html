INTRO

K&M Projects and Logistics (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black owned emerging Micro Enterprise, at B-BBEE Level 1. We are the plant hire service provider of choice. Our company provides all type of equipment plant hire including crane-mounted trucks and similar equipment for the construction and building industry. Our company is also the transporter of choice for road freight specializing in various types of super-link solutions. We provide safe, reliable, dependable and on time road freight solutions to our clients with the highest level of professionalism. Our company also does construction and building projects for both the government departments and private sector, we also supply general services, including supply of general goods.
